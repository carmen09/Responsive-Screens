Welcome to Carmen's Gallery

I could still recall the time when I was just born; that was when my whole family were still living in the countryside of China with limited money. I witnessed how my parents worked really hard to enrich my life ultimately, and it was their hard-working experience that shaped me to be a person like them: whom would spend all the efforts into one thing and persist in it. My parents have also edified me with exposure to traditional Chinese arts since my childhood; for instance, besides learning various Chinese art forms, going to art exhibition monthly is one of our family rituals. In addition, I’m very grateful for my parents because they’ve endowed me with the opportunities to meet famous Chinese artists. Since then, the seeds of pursuing art has sowed not only into my heart but also been planted into my life journey.
